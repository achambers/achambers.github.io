About Me

My name is Aaron Chambers. I'm an Aussie, a fiance and a lover of all things software. I'm a software craftsman living in London. This is my software development (among other things) blog.

I am currently employed by BSkyB. I maintain the crunch-api gem and I wrote the Is The Water Safe To Drink? site. My current obsessions are Ruby and Emberjs.

I love listening to the Ruby Rogues and This American Life podcasts.

If you are on Twitter, you can follow me at @grandazz.

The title of this blog comes from the fact that I am generally a super happy guy. But when I get hungry....well....you better watch out ;)
